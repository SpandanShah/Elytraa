    Score - How to Rank the uni
                University score (of that course), Degree Score
                    -> University Score - Infrastructure score,  Course score, Extra curricular score, Patents score, Fees score, Alumni score
                        -> Infrastructure Score - labs, Premises(land area) and area(which area), sports grounds and equipements.
                        -> Course Score - Faculty score for that perticular Course, Placement score, Curriculum score, relevant electives for perticular course
                            -> Faculty Score - Research Papers, relevance to the course topic (research parper * experience), Patents and Experience, Industry experience, Student Feedback
                            -> Placement score - Avg placement % (from total strength), Placemnt amounts
                            -> Curriculum score - Curriculum related to subjects
                        -> Extra Curricular Course - Fest, activities, functions, workshops
                        -> Patent Score - Number of patents (for ex. 1-3 patents than 10 score)
                        -> Fees score - As per student budget range, and college's scholarship
                        -> Alumni score - Total successful alumni if yes (for ex. 1-3 alumni than 10 score)
